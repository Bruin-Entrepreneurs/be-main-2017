BRUIN ENTREPRENEURS IS

Bruin Entrepreneurs was created in 2012 to promote entrepreneurialism at UCLA. We organize three main programs every year, each with their respective boards: LA Hacks, bVentures, and Creative Labs. Along with these, we host entrepreneurial-related networking events, workshops, and speaker nights every week, designed to spark innovative dialogue between a diverse group of students.

Within the Bruin Entrepreneur family live a variety of different initiatives. We have Creative Labs - for the creatively inclined, Startup Labs - for the students interested in being involved in startups, Tech Fellows - for the students who want to work in the LA startup community, and 1000 pitches - an initiative that is designed to promote entrepreneurial thought thoughout UCLA by getting students to pitch ideas.
